WAC – CAMPUS HERO PROGRAM

SO WHAT IS IT ALL ABOUT?

SEE HOW EASY IT IS

Campus Hero is a program initiated by What After College to encourage new and enthusiastic minds for redefining entrepreneurship. We at What After College provides a platform to the students as well as education stakeholders to earn and learn. Along with it, reaching out to people will also help you strengthen your networking.
A Campus Hero has a great opportunity to earn by selling What After College’s wide range of Product Line of every Education need and creating an impact in their Campus. Responsibilities of a Campus Hero will also include organising various activities in their college.
We would love to work with such great minds who have a vision to create an eco-system where no student will ever be deprived of opportunities.
